Third Campaign

From October 13th to October 20th. The objective was to obtain geoelectric data.

The Chaco-Pampeana region hidrology data obtained in previous campaigns and the satellite images limited the search of paleochannels to certain locations in which prospection was made.

In particular, vertical electrical soundings were made up to 200 meters wide over and around the paleochannels. This allowed the team to determine the apparent resistivity of the area. An electric tomography was also done, this provided major information associated to better resolution in resistivity.
