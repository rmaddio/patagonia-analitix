Who we are?

The issue concerning water resources in the "El Impenetrable‚Äù region is very wide. Water resources are scarce and deeply contaminated. Therefor, intending to affront it implicates approaching the problem with the understanding of various disciplines.

For this reason, several La Plata National University Faculties are colaborating in this project. Each work on their specialty, the "Hard Sciences Faculty" studying the water contamination, "Natural Science and Museum Faculty" and "Astronomical and Geophysical Sciences Faculty" applying prospection methods.

The Student Chapter of the SEG (Society of Exploration Geophysicist) from the "Astronomical and Geophysical Sciences Faculty", headquarter of the project, is also participating.
